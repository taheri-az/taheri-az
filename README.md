## 🔍 About Me

I work on high-level planning and decision-making in unknown environments. My research focuses on bridging formal methods and robotic autonomy to enable reliable behavior in complex, uncertain settings.

- 🧠 Temporal logic-based task planning (LTL, scLTL)
- 🤖 Autonomous systems & robot navigation
- 🔗 Product automata and decision-making under uncertainty
- 👥 Multi-agent systems and coordination

---

## 🚀 Research Focus

My goal is to design provably correct planning frameworks that allow robots to:

- Operate in previously unseen environments
- Adapt to uncertainty and incomplete information
- Satisfy complex temporal specifications
